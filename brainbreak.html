What Are Brain Breaks?

When you're studying continuously for a large amount of time, it's important to take a "brain break," which is a small, relaxing break from the tasks or assignments you were previously focusing on. Small brain breaks can assist during your study and work time by largely reducing stress and frustration while increasing your energy and endurance during your study and work sessions.

Proper brain breaks can help increase your attention and productivity. They can help boost blood flow and send oxygen to the brain to help you retain information better. Some examples of brain breaks may include "focus" games, cardio exercise, a quick walk, or even having a quick chat with someone!
