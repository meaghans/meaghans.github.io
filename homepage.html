Our Inspiration

What made us create this website?

No one expected a pandemic to rule the world, to change everything.

Months have passed and many of us have transitioned to working at home rather than risking our health and working in more social environments surrounded by others.

Many things have changed, and with the continuation of the pandemic, it is extremely important to be able to maintain your health and productivity levels. Thus, we have created this website, as well as an additional extension, to aid students, at-home employees, and many others with remaining focused and productive on their assignments!


The Importance of Productivity

How will this guide help others?

This website not only functions as a guide for improving study and work habits, but it also serves to aid those who work and study at home.

We decided to create this website, as well as an additional extension that promotes the Pomodoro method of efficient studying, to help people around the world adjust to the sudden lifestyle changes brought on by the pandemic. Many of us may struggle with time management, focusing, and getting our work done, so this website addresses those issues with detailed explanations and solutions!


Flow Study Timer

Pace your studying!

The Flow Technique is an efficient method of studying that helps you prevent interruption and distraction while improving your ability to focus! Once the timer begins, it will continue for 45 minutes before changing colors, which will account for the "peak" to reaching your "flow" state of mind. Afterwards, the timer will continue for 90 minutes, which will be the time that you must minimize distractions and completely focus on the task at hand in order to achieve the "flow" state of mind.
