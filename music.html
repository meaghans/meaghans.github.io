The Advantages of Music

Music, especially when used at the right time, can have a great impact on how we think and feel. As for study and work productivity, music is one of the most powerful enhancers for boosting our ability to focus and feel great while we're doing so!

Background music plays a special role in improving our ability to focus on a task by adding to our motivation and feel-good hormone. If you are having a particularly long study session, a little bit of soft background music can assist in helping you study longer.
